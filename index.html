About

A practice between objects, systems, and careful making.

I am interested in design as a way of observing how people meet objects, materials, tools, and spaces. My work often begins with small questions: how a surface is touched, how a part is joined, how a digital instruction becomes a physical trace.

This site collects selected studies rather than presenting a fixed catalogue. It is meant to show a working direction: precise, quiet, iterative, and open to experiments across industrial design and digital fabrication.
