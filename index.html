About

A practice around living objects, material gestures, and home.

Kim Seungbeom designs living objects, lighting, homeware, and small furniture with attention to domestic scale, material tactility, and the quiet ways objects settle into everyday rooms.

This site collects selected studies rather than a fixed catalogue. The work moves between hand observation, material experiments, and digital fabrication as a way to test form, atmosphere, and use.
